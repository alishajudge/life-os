💛 You have seborrheic dermatitis and thinning hair. Consistency is everything — scalp health = hair health. Avoid sulfates, harsh heat, and skipping wash days too long.
🛁 Wash Day Routine
🌿 Scalp Oil Treatment (2-3x/week)
💊 Hair Growth Supplements
Evidence-based for seb derm + thinning:
Biotin — 2500-5000mcg/day (hair + nails)
Iron/Ferritin — low ferritin is the #1 cause of hair loss in young women. Ask your doctor to check ferritin specifically (not just hemoglobin)
Zinc — helps control seb derm + supports hair growth
Vitamin D — deficiency linked to hair loss
Omega-3 — reduces scalp inflammation
📅 Hair Care Schedule
Every 3-4 days: Wash with anti-dandruff shampoo (Nizoral 1% or Head & Shoulders)
2-3x/week: Scalp oil treatment (night before wash)
Weekly: Deep conditioning mask on lengths (avoid scalp)
Limit heat: Air dry when possible. Max heat 300°F with protectant.
Monthly: Trim split ends to prevent breakage from traveling up shaft
🔬 Seb Derm Management
What helps: Ketoconazole shampoo (Nizoral), zinc pyrithione, selenium sulfide, tea tree oil (diluted), salicylic acid scalp serum

What makes it worse: Skipping washes, sweating and not washing, stress, sugary diet, hot showers

Scalp massage: 4 min daily with fingertips (no nails). Proven to increase hair thickness over time. Do it during simran or while studying.

See a dermatologist — seb derm + thinning together may need prescription treatment (topical minoxidil, ketoconazole Rx strength). You deserve real answers.
